This event is open to a lot of different activities, but our focus is on learning about & creating technology projects. Learn to code, create a website, build a robot, or whatever technology learning or project you're interested in.

Conduct

Attendees of this event must read & follow the Open Seattle code of conduct. It's important to us that this is a friendly, safe space for learning.

Background

This monthly event is the successor to Open Seattle's weekly civic hacking night, with a more inclusive range of projects and additional focus on learning about code & technology.
